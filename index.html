The Problem

Generic Policies Are Hurting
Your Store Right Now

In 2024–25, Shopify and payment processors started flagging stores with vague return policies. If your policy doesn't specify return windows, condition rules, and refund timelines — you're leaving yourself exposed.

Shopify can freeze your funds with no warning — and an elevated refund or chargeback rate, caused partly by unclear policies, is one of the most common triggers. A clear, jurisdiction-specific policy reduces disputes before they start.

  • Missing return windows or vague "contact us" language fails payment processor review
  • EU stores without GDPR-aligned returns language face non-compliance risk
  • UK stores must comply with Consumer Contracts Regulations (14-day rule) or risk chargebacks
  • No FAQ means customers email support instead of self-serving — killing your time
  • Copy-pasted template policies get ignored by buyers and flagged by platforms
